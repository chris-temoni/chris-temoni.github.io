I understood the question i was just struggling with what sort of approach i was going to do in terms of how i was going to write the code out and the more i was flicking through the js and test js file the more i was getting confused. so the first thing i did was research for the basics of divisible equation coding (JavaScript). throughout the research i stumbled accross one video that sort of had the exact same approach as the question i was stuck with. what intrigued me about the video was that he talks about the answers being in the questions and if your dealing with files for problem solving the answers are in there also. that gave me some sort of an understanding about what i thought was a question that lacked clarity in relations to kata.
