Emotional intelligence

What is emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence would be having the ability of understanding, managing, and recognizing emotions. It is something that can be developed overtime through self-reflecting, and self-awareness.

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ relates to intelligent related skills which can be classed on various levels. Intelligent related skills will include problem-solving, spatial awareness, memory, and logical reasoning. EQ relates to emotional intelligence with the ability of understanding, managing, and being able to control emotions effectively.

Why is EQ important?

EQ is important because it allows people to manage and understand their emotions the individual is able to gain personal growth, adaptability improved relationships. this can impact different aspects on life.
